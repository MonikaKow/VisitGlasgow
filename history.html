Glasgow


Glasgow boasts numerous galleries, museums and historic buildings and has achieved the status of European City of Culture (1990) and UK City of Architecture and Design (1999). Glasgow's former dockland is the location of Scotland's largest multi-use entertainment venue, the Scottish Exhibition and Conference Centre, which opened in 1985, including the SSE Hydro (added in 2013).
